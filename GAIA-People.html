Priv.-Doz. Dr. Thomas Dreibholz

Thomas's main responsibility is the design, development and management of the NorNet infrastructure. Furthermore, his research focus is on Reliable Server Pooling (RSerPool) as well as particularly on multi-path transport with the Stream Control Transmission Protocol (SCTP) and Multi-Path TCP (MPTCP). He is also involved in standardisation in the IETF.
